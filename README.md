# Job-Analysis-Project-Using-Power-BI-

🚀 **Excited to Share My Latest Power BI Project: AI & ML Jobs Market Analysis Dashboard!**

Over the past few days, I worked on building an interactive dashboard to explore trends in the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning job market. The goal was to transform raw job posting data into meaningful insights that can help job seekers, students, and professionals better understand the industry.

📊 **Key Insights from the Dashboard:**

🔹 **Job Market Overview**

* Analyzed AI & ML job opportunities across multiple roles and locations.
* Identified average salary, median salary, remote work availability, and hiring trends.
* Explored experience-level distribution across Entry, Mid, and Senior positions.

🔹 **Location Insights**

* Compared salaries and job opportunities across countries and cities.
* Visualized geographic distribution of jobs using map-based analytics.
* Highlighted regions offering the highest compensation and hiring demand.

🔹 **Company & Role Analysis**

* Examined company ratings and hiring patterns.
* Compared average salaries across different AI/ML job titles.
* Identified the most valuable and highest-paying roles in the market.

🔹 **Skills & Hiring Trends**

* Analyzed the most in-demand technical skills such as Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, SQL, TensorFlow, and Cloud Computing.
* Explored skill demand by experience level.
* Connected skill requirements with salary and remote work opportunities.

🛠️ **Tools & Technologies Used**

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization Techniques
